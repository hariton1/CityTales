Explore
On this page you can find the interactive map. After selecting a building on the map, it's detailed information will be displayed along with interesting facts and connected building, persons and events. Each detail also includes an interesting fun fact, that you can store and share with your friends!

Tours
Here you can find the preplanned tours as well as plan your on trip in detail. You can also let our application generate best tour for you based on your interests as well as your time or money budget.

Scoreboard
If you want to keep track of your earned XP points, badges or compare your earned points with friends or the whole world, this is the page that you should visit.

Friends
As the name suggest, here you can see your list of friends, but also send and accept the requests from your real-world friends as well as the fellow travelers.

Quizzes
What's better way to verify what have you learned during an interesting tour through the city than doing a little quiz? You can even earn extra points and get an edge over your friends on the leaderboard.

About us
Contact and basic information about the developers, that brought to you this amazing application.

Profile
Here you can change your interests' setting, password and also display the history of your travels and saved fun facts.
